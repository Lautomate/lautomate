Sales Performance

Level: Easy

Tools and Techniques: Excel and Power Query

Topic: Books Sales

I created a dataset that mirrors actual business data, encompassing various aspects of book sales. To ensure the dataset was realistic, I included elements such as sales figures, genres, and customer demographics.

I performed comprehensive data cleaning and transformation processes to replicate the reality of a data analyst's work. This involved handling missing values, correcting inconsistencies, and standardizing formats. A particular challenge was the Discount column, where the values were based on vague criteria. Through meticulous cleaning and transformation, I ensured that the data was accurate and reliable.

I applied all the fundamental data analytics processes to this dataset from asking questions to visualization.

I used the SUMIFS function in Excel to dynamically calculate sales based on various conditions. To determine the current quarter, I employed the TODAY() and ROUNDUP() functions. I also implemented data validation for dropdown menus. Absolute referencing was essential for creating interactive graphs, as it prevents the references from breaking.

Despite the challenges, I managed to deliver exactly what the business wanted and needed. The final dataset and the insights derived from it provided a clear picture of sales performance, helping to inform strategic decisions.
