DAX Basics

Level: Standard

Tools and Techniques: Power BI, DAX, Data Modeling

Topic: Books Sales

I used the cleaned dataset from the sales performance project and enhanced it in Power BI, utilizing various DAX concepts to gain deeper insights. I employed basic DAX functions and concepts, such as operators and operator overloading for arithmetic, logical conditions, and error handling. Relational functions like RELATEDTABLE, combined with aggregators and iterators, were used to dynamically calculate the rank of each product based on performance metrics. I used table constructors to verify data existence under specific criteria and table functions such as FILTER to perform specific data calculations. Additionally, I utilized the Edit Interaction feature to control cross-highlighting and cross-filtering behaviors.

Achieving meaningful and accurate insights would be significantly challenging without a well-structured and effective data modeling process. In this project, I used star schema data modeling, incorporating both fact and dimension tables, to organize and optimize the dataset for analysis.
